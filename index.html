Bio The Arranger

I trained classically on piano, which means the arrangements you get are built on real harmonic understanding, not guesswork — proper voice leading, idiomatic fingerings, and voicings that actually sit well under the hand.

What I do differently: I transcribe by ear. Send me a recording with no sheet music available anywhere, and I'll still get you an accurate, playable score. That's rarer than it sounds.

I work across pop, jazz standards, indie, Disney, cinematic, Christmas, and church/worship repertoire — if you can hum it or send a link, I can probably arrange it.
