Bio About Ali

I'm a concert pianist at the Cairo Opera House, based in Cairo, with a classical training that runs deep. Music has been my life since childhood, and live performance is where I feel most at home.

Today, I bring that same training to weddings, hotels, lounges, and private events — playing live piano that fits the room, from elegant jazz standards to romantic classics to the couple's favorite song.

I'm fully flexible with repertoire, and if there's a specific song you have in mind that I don't already play, I'll arrange and learn it for your event at no extra cost. I also create custom written arrangements and sheet music for singers, students, and other musicians.
