Bio About Ali

I'm a concert pianist and composer at the Cairo Opera House, with a classical training that runs deep — including first prize in the national Chopin Competition in Egypt in 2023 and 2025, and recognition in international competitions including Fujairah and Lalla Meryem.

My work spans original composition for games and media, custom arrangements across any genre, and ear transcription of music with no existing sheet music. I bring the same harmonic sensibility and attention to detail to a game score as I do to a pop arrangement.

I'm available for custom commissions, batch projects, long-term collaboration, and sync licensing. Fast turnaround, clear communication, and original work every time.
